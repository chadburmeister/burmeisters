This is Castle Rock

A growing community — your place to build and expand

This thirty-two acres of undeveloped land is located in the Southwest Quadrant of Castle Rock and is part of an interchange overlay plan designated for commercial development, with building height limits of 90 feet — the equivalent of six stories.

The adjoining forty-two acres to the immediate south is also currently available for development if a larger parcel is required for your project.

With the planned extension of Prairie Hawk Drive south from the Plum Creek interchange with Interstate 25, this property is an ideal location for retail and commercial use, as well as multifamily and mixed use as a buffer to surrounding properties.
